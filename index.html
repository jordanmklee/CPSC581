JORDAN LEE

CPSC 581 Winter 2019

I am Jordan Lee, a computer science student at the University of Calgary. This website serves as a portfolio for the projects I have created for CPSC 581, Human-Computer Interactions II, taken during Winter 2019:




P0 - The Jordan Button

A singular button created to represent the client's (Jordan) personality.





P1 - Smartphone Interaction Workaround

Two animated interfaces as alternatives to smartphone "slide-to-unlock": touch-based dog petting, and sensor-based ball puzzle.





P2 - Solve an Everyday Problem with Arduino

A physical prototype using Arduino to automatically clean paintbrushes.





P3 - Fashion Technology

A fashion tech garment using Arduino that captures the feeling of summer nights at a firework festival.
